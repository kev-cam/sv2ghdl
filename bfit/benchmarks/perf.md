# bfit cross-engine performance

N-stage common-emitter BJT amplifier cascade (`gen_amp.py`), `.tran 20n 2m`.
Each cell is engine **simulation time in seconds** (lower is better) — Windows
engines self-report their "Total elapsed time"; Linux engines are inner
wall-clock, min of 2. Cross-environment process launch is excluded.

The **+bfit** columns substitute the portable `ce_stage` Verilog-AMS macromodel
and take adaptive timesteps. `brk` = engine aborted (timestep collapse on the
stiff deep cascade); `t/o` = exceeded 300s.
